Rome Wasn’t Build in a Day. But Your Website Could Be.

Overnight Site is my latest offering in order to answer a common problem. Your business, your event, or even just your idea, needs a presence on the internet. Having a professional looking web page is vital so that you have a place to give people information, and capture and generate new leads.

Of course, web sites can be expensive. You don’t need anything fancy, and you don’t want to spend a lot, and you don’t want to wait forever. I’m going to let you into a little secret of the web design industry: While some sites do need a customized, optimized design, a whole lot of people and businesses don’t. There are plenty of off the shelf solutions that can have you up and running instantly.

With Overnight Site, we cut out a lot of the unnecessary cruft of long agency design processes, and deliver a site in one business day.

The Process

Step 1: Come Up With a Plan

I want to hit the ground running, so once your day is booked and paid for, we’ll start doing a bit of prep work. I’ll have a brief call with you to discuss content, your brand, and your goals. I’ll send you a few themes to review from our expansive library. We also can provide hosting for an additional charge, or we can help you find hosting that will work for you.

Step 2: Game Day

I’ll contact you at 9 am that day to let you know work has begun. I’ll take the theme & host we decided on, and work on getting it installed. I’ll contact you a few times a day to review content and design changes to make sure we are on the same page. I won’t take up too much of your time, but I’ll need you to be available to give feedback.

Step 3: Start Converting

By the afternoon I’ll be working on integrating other services based on your needs. I’ll set up Google Analytics so you can track your page hits. I’ll set up Mailchimp or any other email provider so you can start building your email list. I’ll integrate with Wufoo and Stripe so you can collecting leads and/or money. Sometimes you can even validate a new idea with a couple simple pages and forms.

No matter what, we want your site to be goal oriented. Whatever your primary business goal is, we want to create a site that will help you accomplish those goals.

At the end of the day, we’ll launch your website.
