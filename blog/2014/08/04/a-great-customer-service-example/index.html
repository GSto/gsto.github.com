How One Blog Post Made Me A Loyal Customer

This post originally appeared on Concordant Solutions. Read why it moved.

I have recently been working on using Nathan Barry’s app ConvertKit to set up some new email marketing tools, and It wasn’t immediately clear to me how to import users, so I sent him an email asking how to do this. In 15 minutes, I get a response that he had just put this up:

A whole blog post, written just for me! I thought it was an impressive effort, and a very quick turnaround. But this is also a good example of another good customer service strategy: Using your blog as your manual / help desk.

Not only did Nathan reach out to me personally, he has a piece of content that accomplishes several other goals:

  • It highlights a feature of his application: ConvertKit can import subscribers from your other applications.
  • It gives him a public piece of content with good keywords, which is good for SEO.
  • If anyone else has the same issue, they may either find this article, or Nathan can just send them a link. Either way, it automates a part of the customer support process, which saves time.
  • It impressed one person so much they decided to blog about it :)

If you are looking for ways to make your customer support more effective and efficient, you could do a lot worse than doing something like this.
