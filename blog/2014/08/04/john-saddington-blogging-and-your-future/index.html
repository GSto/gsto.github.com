John Saddington: Blogging and Your Future

Aug 4th, 2014

Video: John Saddington Talks About How Blogging Changed His Life

This post originally appeared on Concordant Solutions. Read why it moved.

John Saddington gave a talk in February for Transform Athens called “Blogging and your Future”. It was an inspiring talk. I don’t think any talk has gotten me to move to action more so than this one. It was published on Youtube and had a view count way lower than it deserved, so I wanted to share it here:

“Blogging And Your Future” by John Saddington

I’m a big proponent of writing as a tool for not only marketing, but for education and communication as well. I’ve written some more on writing in a couple of other places. I’ve talked on my personal blog about why I share some things on my personal site and some here on my company site. I’ve also written on here on how you shouldn’t stop at blogging, but how you should form an entire content strategy.
