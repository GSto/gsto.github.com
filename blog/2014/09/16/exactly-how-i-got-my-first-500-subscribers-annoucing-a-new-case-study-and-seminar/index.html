Exactly How I Got My First 500 Subscribers: Announcing a New Case Study and Seminar

Sep 16th, 2014 | Comments

Learn The Exact Techniques I Used to Build An Audience From Scratch

Four months ago, I decided to start building an mailing list. I wanted to build new relationships in my field and expand my network. Overtime I built the list to over 500 subscribers. After some conversations, I saw that other people were interested in how I did it. I sat down to write down a case study. I wanted to be as exact and transparent as possible. It got a little out of hand, but at the end I believe I’ve crafted a valuable guide for anyone who wants to start growing their audience.

The Final Result

This case study ended up being much longer than I anticipated. I wanted to be as specific as possible, so that other people could repeat what worked, as well as learn from my mistakes. In addition, I wanted to be transparent. I believe that the best way to teach is to teach everything. After I had collected all of my experiences, I went on to research what other people had done to grow their audience.

The result was over 60 pages of content, in what I originally thought might just be a blog article. I’ve put everything I’ve learned in a PDF, and you can grab it at the end of this article.

Talking About Audience Building Live: September 30th

After putting together this report, I thought it would be fun to talk to others about this in person. So, in addition to publishing this report, I am hosting a live seminar in Athens, GA on Tuesday, September 30th.
