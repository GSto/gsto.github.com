How to Start a Consultancy in 16 Hours From Kai Davis

Sep 18th, 2014

An Actionable Plan From The Side Hustle Nation

My friend Kai Davis was recently on the side hustle nation podcast. The episode is How to Start a Side Hustle Consulting Business that Gets Paid. One particular nugget of gold from this podcast: When asked what he would do if he had to start all over, Kai responds with how he would start a new consultancy in 16 hours.

Hours 1-3: Identify A Target Audience

Decide which market you want to focus on. This could be segmented geographically, or by industry type. If you aren’t sure where to begin, try looking at local business directories. Your local chamber of commerce is a good place to start. You could even look in a phone book if you manage to find one.

Hours 4 - 8: Book Four Calls (But Not Sales Calls)

Start reaching out to people who you think might be potential clients. Ask them to sit down with you over lunch or coffee, or just hop on the phone with you to discuss their business. People love talking about themselves, and even strangers will be more receptive to this than you would believe. Your goal here isn’t to sell them anything, this meeting is research. You want to find the pain points that your potential clients have.

Hours 9 - 12: Begin Crafting A Service

Now that you know what problems people in your target audience are having, how can you solve them? Look for ways you can apply your current skills and knowledge to these problems, or how to apply new skills that you could pick up in a reasonable amount of time. If you don’t find a good fit, that’s OK, you may just have to try again. You don’t marry the first person you date, and you don’t want to pursue the first job idea that comes to mind.

Hours 13 - 16: Offer Your Solution

Now you have connections to your market, and understanding of their problems, and a solution to those problems. Go back to your original contacts and offer your solution. Now you’re in business!
