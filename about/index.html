About Me

My Name is Glenn Stovall, and I run a consulting firm called Concordant Solutions. I named my company Concordant because it symbolizes agreement in harmony. I wanted the project I work on with clients to be the best of the result of the best of their expertise combined with the best of my development knowledge.

What that means is that I don’t like writing 55-page technical specs that no one ever really reads, and then disappear for months at a time while I work through it. Instead, I’m focused on client involvement and constant communication. I always want you to know what’s going on with your project and what’s going to happen next.

So at the end of the day, I’m not looking to be a developer that blindly builds whatever is asked of him. I’m looking to be a partner to help people bring their ideas to life with as great a chance of success as possible.
